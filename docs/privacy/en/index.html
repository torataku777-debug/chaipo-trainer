This Privacy Policy describes how Toratani Taku (hereinafter referred to as "the Developer") handles user information in the mobile application "OFC Neighbors" (hereinafter referred to as "the App"). Please read this policy carefully before using the App.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect any personally identifiable information from users. Furthermore, the App alone does not transmit any information to external servers.

To enhance user convenience and provide a continuous gameplay experience, the following information is stored only on your device's local storage:

  • App settings (time limits, starting score, and other game configurations)
  • Play logs and match history
  • Statistics related to Fantasy Land practice
  • Season start time
  • Other information necessary for game progress

This data never leaves your device, and no third parties — including the Developer — can access or view it.

2. Sharing of Information with Third Parties

The App does not share any user information with any third party (including other users, companies, organizations, or individuals).

3. Advertising, Analytics, and Crash Reporting

The App does NOT use any of the following services or SDKs:

  • Advertising SDKs (AdMob, etc.)
  • Analytics tools (Google Analytics, Mixpanel, etc.)
  • Crash reporting tools (Firebase Crashlytics, Sentry, etc.)
  • User tracking (IDFA, etc.)

4. Data Deletion

You can delete data stored on your device using either of the following methods:

  • Use the data management feature in the App's "Settings" screen
  • Uninstall the App, which will delete all related data

5. Children's Privacy

The App is offered to a general audience without age restrictions. Since the App does not collect personal information, it can be used by children without parental consent. However, if you have any concerns about your child's use of the App, please supervise their usage at your discretion.

6. In-App Purchases

The App (as of v1.0) does not offer in-app purchases. If in-app purchases are introduced in the future, this Privacy Policy will be revised to clearly state how purchase-related information is handled.

7. Future Updates

Future versions of the App may add server-connected features such as online matches. When such features are enabled, this Privacy Policy will be revised to clearly state the types of additional information handled, the purposes of use, and retention periods. Users will be notified of any such changes in the revised version of the App.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Policy may be changed without notice in response to legal amendments, changes in service contents, etc. When making important changes, we will provide advance notice on this page and within the App. The revised Policy will apply once it takes effect.
